Cost of Goods Sold (COGS) Report

Sales & POS Integrated | Date-wise Cost Tracking

This module provides a powerful report to track the total cost of goods sold from both Sales and Point of Sale (POS). Easily analyze your product costs with a clean and user-friendly interface.

Key Features

  • ✔ Generate Cost of Goods Sold (COGS) report
  • ✔ Date-wise filtering for accurate analysis
  • ✔ Integrated with Sales module
  • ✔ Integrated with POS module
  • ✔ Clean and easy-to-use interface

How It Works

The module calculates the cost of sold products based on the product cost price. It aggregates data from Sales Orders and POS Orders and generates a detailed report based on the selected date range.
